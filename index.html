📋 Technical Documentation

Algorithm: Logistic regression (ridge penalty) blended with Elo ratings (K=20). Features: offensive/defensive efficiency, explosiveness, havoc rates, finishing drives, tempo, field position, spread-implied probability, home field advantage, travel distance, rest days.

Calibration: Platt scaling applied post-model. Probabilities clamped [0.05, 0.95].

Data Sources:

  • GET /calendar - Current week detection
  • GET /games - Schedule and matchups
  • GET /lines - Vegas spreads and totals
  • GET /stats/season - Team season stats
  • GET /stats/game - Game-level stats
  • GET /ratings/elo - Elo ratings
  • GET /teams - Team metadata
