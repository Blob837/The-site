Our Story

Built on calibration, not hype.

Praeco began with a simple constraint: prediction quality must be measured, audited, and improved—continuously. Our team built trading and scheduling systems that lived or died on calibration. "Praeco"—Latin for "herald"—captures our job: announce tomorrow's odds with the humility of uncertainty. We ship models that explain themselves, improve with scrutiny, and earn trust the hard way—over time.
