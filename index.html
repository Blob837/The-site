Story

Founded in Houston

Built by Engineers, Calibrated by Reality

Praeco was founded in Houston by engineers who shipped systems that lived or died on calibration. We start where outcomes are scored against reality—production, flow, basis—and we publish the confidence, not just the number. 'Praeco'—Latin for 'herald'—captures our job: announce tomorrow's odds with the humility of uncertainty. We earn trust with audits, drift reports, and better calibration each month.

Roadmap

Product Timeline

Methodical expansion from Texas to national coverage.

Q4 2025: Texas Basins GA

On Track

Scope: Permian, Delaware, Midland, Eagle Ford production models with calibrated intervals. APIs for batch and real-time inference.

Success Metrics: MAPE <8%, coverage >95% of active rigs, latency <500ms for live endpoints.

Risk: Late-arriving field data; mitigation via imputation + confidence penalties.

Q1 2026: Broader U.S. Basin Onboarding

Planned

Scope: Haynesville, Bakken, DJ, Powder River. Extend model stack to new geologies and regulatory contexts.

Success Metrics: Maintain MAPE <8% across all basins, sub-second API response times.

Risk: Data quality variance; mitigation via basin-specific feature engineering.

Q2 2026: Expanded Commodity Curves + Storage

Planned

Scope: Refined products, NGLs, storage/inventory signals. Curve modeling for forward prices.

Success Metrics: Basis prediction error <5%, inventory forecasts within 10% of actuals.

Risk: Market regime shifts; mitigation via adaptive retraining and ensemble diversity.

Q3 2026: SOC 2 Type I

Planned

Scope: Complete SOC 2 Type I audit, formalize security and compliance processes.

Success Metrics: Clean audit, zero critical findings, documented controls.

Risk: Audit timeline slips; mitigation via early engagement with auditors.

Q4 2026: EU Commodity Exposure + Multilingual

Planned

Scope: Brent, TTF, European refined products. Multilingual dashboards and documentation.

Success Metrics: Launch in 3+ EU markets, localized UI/UX, compliance with GDPR.

Risk: Regulatory complexity; mitigation via local partnerships and legal counsel.
