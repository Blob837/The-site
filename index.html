    About

    This app analyzes news articles for political bias using client-side lexicons and scoring. Scores range from -100 (left-leaning) to +100 (right-leaning). The method employs lexicon-based feature counting, sentiment analysis around political entities, and optional weak outlet priors for enhanced accuracy.
